Apply to become a Hope Hub counsellor

We are building an accessible mental wellness support network for people who need calm, respectful, and privacy-aware guidance. Share your profile and our team will review fit, availability, and service areas.

Who should apply?

  • Qualified counsellors, psychologists, therapists, or supervised trainees.
  • People comfortable with online support, chat follow-up, and community guidance.
  • Professionals who respect confidentiality, boundaries, and crisis escalation limits.

Selection Steps

  1. 1. Submit your counsellor application.
  2. 2. Team reviews qualification, experience, and availability.
  3. 3. Shortlisted profiles are contacted for verification.
  4. 4. Approved counsellors are onboarded for Hope Hub support flows.
